Reading the chart

Each stacked layer shows the cumulative percent of CONUS that was at least in that drought category for a given week. Notice the persistent dry years of 2002 and 2012, where D4 (exceptional drought, dark red) reached above 6% nationally — among the most severe events in the USDM record.
