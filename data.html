Data

Download U.S. Drought Monitor data in a format that fits your work — comprehensive statistics, the Drought Severity and Coverage Index (DSCI), counts of weeks in drought, population in drought, threshold queries, GIS shapefiles, and metadata.

📈

Comprehensive Statistics

All USDM categories for every week of a selected time period and location. Options include percent of area, total area, percent of population, and total population.

📊

DSCI Statistics

Drought Severity and Coverage Index — a single value summarizing both severity and extent for a given area and date range.

Open DSCI Statistics →

Statistics by Threshold

Pick a place, time, and minimum or maximum drought level — get the proportion of that area meeting your criteria.

Open Statistics by Threshold →

Weeks In Drought

How many weeks has an area been in each USDM category? Track cumulative drought weeks at the threshold of your choice.

Open Weeks In Drought →

Data Tables

The week's USDM statistics displayed as sortable tables — by state, by county, or by climate region.

Open Data Tables →
📉

Data Graphs & Time Series

Stacked area charts of percent area in each USDM category over time, for any region from 2000 to present.

GIS Data
